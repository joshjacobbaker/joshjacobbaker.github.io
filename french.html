"He was as you and I, flesh and blood, breathing and thinking.
He bore the burdens we bare. He was tempted by the temptations we've been tempted by.
His friends and family, watched He was socially mocked and ridiculed, and laughed at.
He was whipped by a cat of nine-tails, embedded with leaded balls, shards of glass and metal.
Nearly naked and dying, he carried a cross up the streets, while on lookers were either horrified or elated.
An african was ordered to help him carry the cross as He began to fall, bleeding internally, and aproaching death.
If He was God--what have we done? It wasn't JUST those who were present, but it's ME, you, all of us.
I don't know why everything is perfect, cause I'm not, but this, this is, and we're lucky to be a part of it.
If there was a God, this God would obviously be CRAZY AWESOME and highly intelligent--do we not see intelligence?

I can articulately outline SO MUCH more, to better explain WHY I believe in JESUS as YHWH, the God of the Universe
but I still have so many questions, for now, it's time for us to humble ourselves, and continue asking questions.
